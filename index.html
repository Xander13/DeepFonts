    Instructions

    ❮ ❯

    Insert


    Find fonts you like in the font list you wish to add into your Queue. You need at lease 2 fonts in order to make new fonts.


    When you find your fonts of your choice, you will see your fonts on the left panel of the queue.

    Watch


    Take a step back and watch the ML create the new fonts! You can reorder the list to prioritize what font face you like and it will change the font to your liking.

    Print


    You can export your custom font to web font or poster font. Click save to save in your downloads and you will have a zip file of your custom font you have created.
